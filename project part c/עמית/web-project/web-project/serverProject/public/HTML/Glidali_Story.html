Glidali's Story

Glidli Ice Cream was established in Israel in 1998
and was the first Italian ice cream in Israel
A group of friends from Ben Gurion University who decided to
fulfill a dream and establish a quality ice cream near Be'er Sheva University

We use only fresh materials from the very top quality.
We create our legendary flavors with the best toppings
while combine seasonal fruits.




We do not compromise on the taste!
